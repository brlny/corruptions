Where to Access Reports and Public Information

Citizens can use several platforms to review how public funds are being spent:

  • COA Audit Reports – Access official government financial reports.
  • Freedom of Information (FOI) Portal – Request public documents online.
  • Local Government Websites – View budget updates and project details in your area.

These tools allow the public to monitor government spending and ensure officials remain transparent.
