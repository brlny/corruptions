Hotlines and Reporting Channels

If you wish to report corruption or seek assistance, you may contact:

  • Office of the Ombudsman – for complaints against public officials
  • Commission on Audit (COA) – for concerns on government spending
  • PNP / DILG – for local corruption or misuse of public funds
  • Local Government Units in Zambales – for community-level issues

Speaking up helps protect public resources and strengthens transparency across the province.
