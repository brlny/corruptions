How Citizens Can Participate

Every citizen plays a role in promoting honesty and accountability. You can help by:

  • Joining barangay and municipal meetings
  • Asking questions about local budgets and projects
  • Using transparency tools to verify information
  • Reporting suspicious activities to proper authorities

Active citizens strengthen democracy and help build a trustworthy and responsible government.
